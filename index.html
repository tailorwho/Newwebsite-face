About A+Students

A+Students is a leading franchise in the education sector that offers abacus and mental arithmetic training, combined with school mathematics and a range of A+Play Maths products and games. Our structured courses cater to students from 36 months and continue well into teenage years, ensuring lasting knowledge and training imparted on the student. Our founder, Marlene Mouton, is a former mathematics and science teacher who started A+Students to help struggling math students. We are the only institution that offers abacus (Soroban) education combined with school mathematics and A+Play Maths, and we offer profitable franchise opportunities with a high return on investment. Contact us to learn more about our courses and franchise opportunities.
